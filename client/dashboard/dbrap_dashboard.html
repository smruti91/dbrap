Government of India · DPIIT

District Business Reform Action Plan

DBRAP is a strategic localization of the national Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP), launched by DPIIT. Its primary goal is to improve the Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) at the grassroots level by tracking credit-linked scheme performance across districts.

PMEGP
Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme — supports micro-enterprises via KVIC portal under MoMSME.
MUDRA Loans
Shishu, Kishor & Tarun tiers for micro-unit financing up to ₹10 lakh, managed by DFS.
PM SVANidhi
Working capital loans for street vendors to resume livelihoods, managed by MoHUA.
SHG Bank Linkage
Self Help Group financial inclusion through Mission Shakti & BLIS portal in Odisha.
Kisan Credit Card
Timely agricultural credit to farmers via SAFAL portal managed by Agriculture & FE Dept, Odisha.
EoDB Objective
Reduce approval timelines, improve transparency, and monitor district banking compliance at scale.
